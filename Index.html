How the Institutional Engine Works
Step 1 — Structure
6H Bias Direction
Higher timeframe structure defines directional bias. Only bullish setups in uptrend, bearish in downtrend — mirroring institutional macro positioning.
Step 2 — Map Zones
Mark Institutional Zones
Order blocks, FVGs, and sweep levels mapped from 6H/1H. The bot arms the trigger and waits — it never chases price.
Step 3 — Mitigation
Price Returns to Zone
Institutions re-accumulate at their own levels. When price revisits the zone, vigilance state activates and confluence is evaluated.
Step 4 — Score
ICS Computed Live
All six confluence factors evaluated simultaneously. Composite ICS score 0–100 computed. Below threshold → bot stands aside entirely.
Step 5 — OTE Entry
Precision Entry
Entry at 50% of order block (OTE). Stop beyond institutional zone. TP at opposing liquidity pool. Min R:R 1:3 enforced.
Step 6 — Audit Log
Tagged and Stored
Every signal stored with ICS score, zone type, entry rationale, and institutional classification. Full audit trail.
